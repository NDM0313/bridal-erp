# 30-Day Adoption Plan

**Purpose**: Lock ERP usage as a habit, not just software  
**Goal**: ERP becomes single source of truth, old system fully stopped

---

## Week 1: Focus on Discipline (Days 1-7)

### Goal
**Get everyone using ERP every day, even if imperfect.**

### Daily Focus
- ✅ **Login every day** - No exceptions
- ✅ **Complete daily tasks** - Even if slow
- ✅ **Follow checklists** - Don't skip steps
- ✅ **Report issues** - Don't suffer in silence

### What to Ignore
- ❌ **Don't worry about perfection** - Focus on usage
- ❌ **Don't compare with old system** - Focus on ERP
- ❌ **Don't get frustrated with slowness** - It will improve

### Owner Actions
1. **Monitor daily** - Check if everyone is logging in
2. **Encourage usage** - Praise those using ERP
3. **Address issues quickly** - Don't let problems pile up
4. **Be patient** - First week is learning phase

### Success Criteria
- ✅ All users login daily
- ✅ All users complete their tasks
- ✅ No one reverts to old system
- ✅ Issues are reported and addressed

---

## Week 2: Fix Common Mistakes (Days 8-14)

### Goal
**Fix mistakes, reinforce correct usage.**

### Daily Focus
- ✅ **Identify common mistakes** - What keeps going wrong?
- ✅ **Fix mistakes immediately** - Don't let them repeat
- ✅ **Reinforce correct usage** - Show right way
- ✅ **Provide additional training** - If needed

### Common Mistakes to Fix
1. **Wrong branch selection** - Remind to check branch
2. **Not finalizing sales** - Remind to finalize
3. **Not updating production steps** - Remind to update
4. **Skipping cost entry** - Remind to enter costs

### Owner Actions
1. **Review daily operations** - Find patterns of mistakes
2. **Provide quick fixes** - Address mistakes immediately
3. **Reinforce training** - Remind correct procedures
4. **Celebrate improvements** - Praise correct usage

### Success Criteria
- ✅ Common mistakes are reduced
- ✅ Correct usage is reinforced
- ✅ Team is more comfortable
- ✅ Fewer issues reported

---

## Week 3: Speed + Confidence (Days 15-21)

### Goal
**Work faster, build confidence, reduce supervision.**

### Daily Focus
- ✅ **Work faster** - Less time per task
- ✅ **Build confidence** - Less hesitation
- ✅ **Reduce supervision** - Team works independently
- ✅ **Improve accuracy** - Fewer mistakes

### Speed Improvements
1. **Sales creation** - Should be faster now
2. **Production updates** - Should be quicker
3. **Cost entry** - Should be routine
4. **Dashboard checks** - Should be routine

### Owner Actions
1. **Reduce supervision** - Let team work independently
2. **Monitor from distance** - Check dashboard, not over shoulder
3. **Trust the team** - They know what to do
4. **Focus on business** - Not on system usage

### Success Criteria
- ✅ Tasks completed faster
- ✅ Team works independently
- ✅ Less supervision needed
- ✅ Confidence is high

---

## Week 4: ERP as Single Source of Truth (Days 22-30)

### Goal
**ERP is the only system, old system fully stopped.**

### Daily Focus
- ✅ **ERP only** - No old system usage
- ✅ **All data in ERP** - Nothing outside
- ✅ **Trust ERP data** - Don't double-check with old system
- ✅ **ERP is habit** - Natural, not forced

### Final Steps
1. **Stop old system completely** - No exceptions
2. **Archive old records** - Move to storage, not daily use
3. **Trust ERP data** - Use ERP for all decisions
4. **ERP is normal** - Not special, just how we work

### Owner Actions
1. **Enforce ERP-only rule** - No old system allowed
2. **Trust ERP data** - Make decisions from ERP
3. **Celebrate adoption** - Recognize team success
4. **Plan for long-term** - ERP is permanent

### Success Criteria
- ✅ Old system not used
- ✅ All data in ERP
- ✅ ERP is natural habit
- ✅ Team fully adopted

## Warning Signs (Watch For)

### Week 1 Warning Signs
- ⚠️ Users not logging in
- ⚠️ Users reverting to old system
- ⚠️ Too many complaints
- ⚠️ Too many errors

**Action**: Address immediately, provide support

### Week 2 Warning Signs
- ⚠️ Same mistakes repeating
- ⚠️ No improvement
- ⚠️ Users still confused
- ⚠️ Training not helping

**Action**: Review training, provide additional help

### Week 3 Warning Signs
- ⚠️ Still slow
- ⚠️ Still need supervision
- ⚠️ Still making mistakes
- ⚠️ Still not confident

**Action**: Review processes, identify bottlenecks

### Week 4 Warning Signs

- ⚠️ Don't trust ERP data
- ⚠️ ERP feels forced
- ⚠️ Not natural habit
